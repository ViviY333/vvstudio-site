NOX Ventures — Full-Lifecycle Crypto Incubation Fund

2025.8-2026
Overview

NOX Ventures is a full-lifecycle crypto incubation fund. Its name derives from the Latin "nox" (night), symbolizing the discovery of potential within the unknown. vvstudio crafted a comprehensive brand ecosystem spanning strategy, visual identity, digital experience, and marketing touchpoints.

Challenge

1. How to establish a brand that is professionally credible yet boldly cutting-edge in Web3?

2. How to balance financial rigor with crypto culture's rebellious spirit?

3. How to design a scalable system adaptable from institutional roadshows to community operations?

Brand Strategy

Brand philosophy, mission and vision—positioning NOX as the strategic co-founder for high-potential crypto teams with end-to-end guidance from strategy to execution.

Visual Identity

Logo, color system, typography showcase—systematic asset library for internal teams and partners.

Landing Page

WebGL-powered immersive hero, ASCII effects, layered parallax that unfolds investment philosophy on scroll. Performance balanced with visual impact.

Marketing

Extended the brand across every digital touchpoint: Twitter profile, headers, investor pitch decks, and social content templates.

Outcome

Within 3 months of launch, NOX Ventures successfully closed its $20M first fund and expanded its portfolio to 10 early-stage projects including Sign, Surge, and others.
